LAYOUT SYSTEM


Recycling Article


The Content of the article refers to reusing and re-cycling old materials. I was trying to uplift this idea by picking the yellow colour to bring ideals of joy and inspiration, which arises from the link to design. I created a plan using a single typeface over the whole article to entrench the design unity of the article furthered by the central design motif of an uplifting bright layout.
