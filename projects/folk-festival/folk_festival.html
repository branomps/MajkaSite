ICON SYSTEM


Folk Festival


I have created a set of five icons for the Slovakian folk festival- The Unforgettable Craft. As a focal point of communications I used hands, which symbolize hard handmade work. I attempted to model a simple design, but purely easily readable icons with repeating elements to link the design. The inspiration for my color pallet was typical Slovakian folk costumes.
